Drones, drones, drones!

Whether you're a nitro-fuelled racer, an acrobatic freestyler, or enjoy the leisured pace of drone cinematography, the West Wales Drone Club is the place for you! Situated in the idyllic countryside of West Wales, we feature race days on our race courses, freestyle events, cinematography competitions, and are a great place to share tips and tricks all things drone.
